## Scope

- Importing and organizing input data (geometry, profile, pavement layers)
- Building a 3D model in a format usable by the contractor
- Consistency validation and packaging data for next stages

## Result

The 3D model is ready to:

- feed construction machinery (machine guidance),
- support volume analyses,
- support cost estimation and change control.

## Tech

`Python` • `GIS/BIM` • `automation` • `GeoTIFF/XYZ` (depending on input sources)
